Media Codes Cheat Sheet

  • Technical codes – camera (shots, angles, movement), editing, sound, lighting.
  • Symbolic codes – body language, facial expression, costume, props, colour, setting.
  • Written codes – titles, captions, on-screen text, fonts, slogans.
  • Audio – diegetic (in the world) vs non-diegetic (added for the audience).
  • Denotation – what you actually see or hear.
  • Connotation – what it suggests or makes the audience think/feel.

Exam tip: high-band answers clearly name the code and then explain its effect on the audience.
