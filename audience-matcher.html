Audience Cheat Sheet

  • Demographics – age, gender, income, job, education.
  • Interests – hobbies, fandoms, genres they enjoy.
  • Lifestyle – how they spend their time (gaming, streaming, sport, commuting).
  • Access – devices and platforms they use (phone, console, TV, social media).
  • Rating & suitability – PEGI / BBFC ratings, school rules, parents’ expectations.

Exam tip: when you describe a target audience, include age range, gender (if relevant), key interests and how / where they would access the product.
