9-Marker Power Moves

  • Structure: clear intro, organised middle, mini-conclusion.
  • Balance: consider more than one side (advantages / disadvantages / options).
  • Explain impact: link each point to audience, purpose or client needs.
  • Language: use connectives – “however”, “therefore”, “as a result”.
  • Examples: refer to the scenario – name the product, audience and platform.
  • Judgement: if it says “evaluate”, give a justified overall opinion.

Exam tip: a Band 3 answer is not just longer – it explains, compares and justifies.
