Legal & Ethical Cheat Sheet

  • Copyright – protects original work (images, video, audio, code).
  • Trademark – protects logos, brand names and slogans.
  • Data Protection / GDPR – controls how personal data is collected, stored and used.
  • Classification – BBFC (films), PEGI (games) give age ratings.
  • Regulators – ASA (adverts), Ofcom (broadcast & on-demand).
  • Consent – permission to use people’s images, voices or locations.

Tip: many R093 questions ask you to identify a law then explain how it affects a media product.
