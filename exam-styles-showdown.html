Exam Style Cheat Sheet

  • Identify – name a single fact (usually 1 mark).
  • Describe – give 2–3 clear points with some detail.
  • Explain – point and reason / impact (use “because”, “so that”).
  • Extended – several linked points, often comparing ideas and linking back to the brief.

Tip: before you write, underline the command word and the number of marks. Plan one strong point for each mark.
