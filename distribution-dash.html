Distribution Cheat Sheet

  • Traditional platforms – cinema, TV, radio, print, billboards.
  • Digital platforms – websites, streaming services, apps, social media.
  • On-demand – watch/listen when the audience chooses (catch-up, streaming).
  • Cross-media promotion – using more than one platform to advertise the same product.
  • Scheduling – choosing times/slots that match the target audience (e.g. after school, prime time).
  • Targeting – matching platform and format to age, interests and access (mobile, console, smart TV, etc.).

Exam tip: when explaining a choice, mention the platform, the audience and how it helps the product reach or engage that audience.
