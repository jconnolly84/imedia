Script Health Checklist

        
    
          
  • Layout – scene headings, margins, clear speaker names.
    • 
          
  • Directions – camera, action and sound in brackets or separate columns.
    • 
          
  • Dialogue – what characters say, in the correct column/line.
    • 
          
  • Audio – music, SFX and voiceover clearly labelled.
    • 
          
  • Timing – approximate timings can be included for adverts/trailers.
    • 
        

        

          Exam tip: when asked to describe a script, mention layout (columns, labels) AND content (dialogue, directions, sounds).
