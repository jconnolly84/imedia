Pre-production Toolkit

  • Client brief – requirements agreed with the client.
  • Mood board – style, colours and inspiration.
  • Mind map – organise and expand ideas.
  • Visualisation diagram – what a still product will look like.
  • Storyboard – sequence of shots for video/animation.
  • Script – dialogue, actions and direction.
  • Wireframe – layout and navigation of screens.
  • Gantt chart – tasks and timings across a project.
  • Asset table – list of assets, sources and permissions.
